Life's Journey Soundscapes

"Humanity begins to turn Dark and Gray like a stone cold society."

This collection of songs serves as a raw, chronological map of a profound emotional journey. It begins in the quiet echo of total loneliness, documenting the descent into a period of life so bleak it felt like an absolute void. The narrative shifts with the arrival of a girl, an encounter that initially promised light but ultimately spiraled into a labyrinth of total lies and betrayal. What remains is a haunting transition into "nightly dreams," where the music explores the surreal space between what was lost and the lingering subconscious visions that follow such a transformative heartbreak.

ENTER THE SOUND

This production was a unique fusion of analog power and digital precision, built on the backbone of a Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon 4th Gen running Ubuntu 25.10. I tracked the heart of the music using my personal 15-piece Pearl Export drum set, capturing the raw energy of a massive acoustic kit, while various AI engines were leveraged to generate sophisticated vocal performances and intricate fills that complemented the live percussion.

The entire project was helmed in Ardour, where I arranged the tracks and utilized FFMPEG via the command line to arrange the track, also add levels of bass, depth, and character to the raw inputs. To finalize the aesthetic and technical details, I designed the album artwork in GIMP, managed the metadata and ID3 tags with Kid3 to ensure everything was properly categorized, and used VLC as my primary reference monitor for playback.

Key Tools: Pearl Export (15-pc) Acoustic Kit • Ardour • FFMPEG • GIMP • Kid3 • VLC
